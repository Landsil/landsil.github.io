The High Five Game illustrates the randomness involved in teaching tricks to a cat. The project's inspiration is called Jack; he's been learning this command since he was a kitten, but the results of any given exercise are hit-and-miss, with a treat increasing your odds. Jack thinks "high five" means "free treats", and is there anything wrong with that?

The game has a hidden motivation stat; offering the hand randomly decreases the cat's motivation, while offering treats randomly increases it. But be careful not to overfeed!
